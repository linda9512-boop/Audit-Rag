Audit question: Is each device uniquely identified and linked to its technical documentation?

================================================================================
The audit question asks whether each device is uniquely identified and linked to its technical documentation. Based on the retrieved excerpts, the following information is relevant:

1. **Unique Device Identifier (UDI):**
   - The HAL 4.0 device is equipped with a Unique Device Identifier (UDI) that includes the manufacturer, model number, serial number, and date of manufacture as stated in the UDI requirements provided on the hardware nameplate [Design Input Specification / System Requirement Specifications / D63662 HAL 4.0 System Requirements Specification rev 02.pdf | Page: 837].
   - Specific UDIs for Halcyon 4.0 are also listed for EU MDR compliance and model identification [Design Output Specification / Design Descriptions / D58308 HAL 4.0 Device Description Rev 1.0.docx.pdf | Page: 4].

2. **Link to Technical Documentation:**
   - The UDI format is implemented, ensuring that the device can be identified and linked with its accompanying documents [Regulatory / Test Reports - Regulatory Compliance / D101279 106485676MIN-004 IEC60601-1-3 Ed.2.2 HAL4.0_30_APR_2026 Rev 1.pdf | Page: 10].
   - Accompanying documents include necessary UDIs and identify the items they refer to [Regulatory / Test Reports - Regulatory Compliance / 102341812MIN-003a - HAL4.0 - IEC 60601-1-3.pdf | Page: 9].
   - The device serial number and other identifying attributes are documented in technical specifications, linking the hardware to the documentation [Design Output Specification / D65617 HAL4 DCS Rev 02.docx.pdf | Page: 61].

These documents collectively establish that HAL 4.0 is uniquely identified by its UDI and that this identifier is linked to its technical documentation.
