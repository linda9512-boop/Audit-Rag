Audit question: Are all devices, including accessories, software, variants, and families, appropriately identified and included?
Chunks used: 105
================================================================================
Based on the provided document excerpts, the information regarding the identification and inclusion of devices, accessories, software, variants, and families within the HAL 4.0 radiotherapy system is as follows:

1. **Devices and System Identification**:
   - The document [Design Output Specification / Design Descriptions / D46704 HAL 4.0 Accessory and Component List Rev 1.0.docx.pdf | Page: 3] specifies that the HAL 4.0 includes accessories and components for all models of linear accelerators within the Halcyon platform, which includes Halcyon and Ethos radiotherapy systems.

2. **Software and Version Identification**:
   - A process to ensure product versions and builds for Varian and third-party devices installed in test environments is outlined in [Design Validation / Verification and Validation Summary Reports / D65886 HAL 4.0 Validation Scorecard Rev 02.docx.pdf | Page: 19]. It includes an Engineering Release Memo identifying all products, versions, and builds, with the requirement for specific version and build numbers to be identified by the Verification Test Readiness Review (TRR).
   - The [Regulatory / Test Reports - Regulatory Compliance / D101277 106485676MIN-001 IEC60601-1 Ed.3.2 HAL4.0_30_APR_2026 Rev 1.pdf | Page: 284-285] specifies that the manufacturer establishes a scheme for unique identification of configuration items and their versions as part of the software configuration management process.

3. **Accessories**:
   - The document [Regulatory / International Submission Dossier / D70060 HAL 4.0 GSPR Checklist Rev2.docx.pdf | Page: 34-35] notes that there are no accessories required with HAL, as stated in the Customer Release Note.

4. **Process and List for Configuration Management**:
   - The [Regulatory / Regulatory Plans / D54978 HAL 4.0 Regulatory Plan Rev 5.0.pdf | Page: 65-66] requires a separated list of all accessories for exclusive use, consumables, and optionals, including codes and pictures, to comply with the Brazilian regulation. This ensures that all relevant accessories are documented and included.

The documents collectively identify the main components and accessories related to the HAL 4.0 radiotherapy system but notably mention that there are no additional accessories required for HAL, as per the Customer Release Note. However, the comprehensive identification of all device versions, builds, and accessories seems partially covered; there is no explicit mention of variants or families in the excerpts provided.
