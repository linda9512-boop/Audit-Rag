The organization ensures updates to regulatory requirements are reflected in technical documentation through a structured approach as evident in the following excerpts:

1. **Compliance Updates**: The Halcyon 4.0 Regulatory Plan outlines several instances where compliance updates are required for various standards and documents. These requirements indicate that the organization tracks which documents need updates based on revised standards. For example, updates are required for compliance with standards like IEC 60601-1, IEC 60601-1-2, IEC 60601-2-68, and specific documentation such as the Summative Usability Test Report [Regulatory / Regulatory Plans / D54978 HAL 4.0 Regulatory Plan Rev 5.0.pdf, pages 40-41].

2. **Document Update Responsibility**: The Regulatory Plan highlights responsibilities for updating specific documents, including the Statement of Indications for Use and Device Description to align with the latest regulatory requirements or templates [Regulatory / Regulatory Plans / D54978 HAL 4.0 Regulatory Plan Rev 5.0.pdf, page 38]. This shows that specific roles within the organization are tasked with ensuring documents are updated as per current regulatory needs.

3. **Submission Documentation**: The plan specifies that all International Submission Dossier (ISD) documentation must be submitted electronically as they are approved and notes that additional documents may be required depending on evolving EU Regulatory requirements [01. Design and Development Planning / 01. System / D30786 Halcyon 2.0 Regulatory Plan Rev 1.0.doc.pdf, page 50].

4. **Regular Monitoring and Adaptation**: The organization maintains a process for monitoring regulatory markets during projects and communicates changes to the team for adaptation of documentation, as shown in the Brazil Marketing Clearance Strategy [01. Design and Development Planning / 01. System / D30786 Halcyon 2.0 Regulatory Plan Rev 1.0.doc.pdf, page 63]. 

These processes collectively demonstrate that Varian Medical Systems uses its regulatory planning and management strategies to align technical documentation with updated regulatory requirements. However, the provided excerpts primarily detail the requirement and responsibility for updates rather than describing a complete process or a system in active place for accomplishing this routinely across all documentation.
